iCloud identity

privacy

your data stays
yours.

Built on Apple CloudKit. No external database, no password to remember, no account to create. Your iCloud account is all you need.

Apple CloudKit
All data lives in your private iCloud database. Apple's infrastructure, Apple's privacy standards.
no passwords
Identity is derived from your iCloud account. No sign-up form, no email, no password handling.
user-isolated data
Every record is scoped to your iCloud account. No data is shared between users.
Apple ecosystem
Follows Apple's privacy-first design patterns. No external auth database required for any core functionality.
